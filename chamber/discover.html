Overview

Eko, the commercial hub of Lagos Island. Located right in heart of Lagos state the largest commercial metropolis of Nigeria. Sprawling inland from the Gulf of Guinea across Lagos Lagoon. The home to the National Museum, where our cultural artifacts and craftworks are displayed, and the Freedom Park, once a colonial-era prison. But now a major venue for concerts and public events.
